Zion’s misc notes

This archive taught me that longing is repetitive. Everyone thinks their heartbreak is unique, but the language overlaps. Timing. Distance. Emotional unavailability. Fear disguised as logic.

Reading these notes made me realize how often people apologize for wanting to be chosen.

I learned that screenshots are modern love letters. That ambiguity hurts more than rejection. That closure is usually self-written.
